# CHA 11.05

Prove that $\left| \mathbb{Z} \right| = \left| \mathbb{Z} - \{ 2 \} \right|$.

---

## Proof

By Result 11.03, $\mathbb{Z}$ is denumerable. Observe that $\mathbb{Z} - \{ 2 \}$ is an infinite subset of $\mathbb{Z}$. By Theorem 11.04, every infinite subset of a denumerable set is denumerable. Therefore, $\mathbb{Z} - \{ 2 \}$ is denumerable. Thus, $\left| \mathbb{Z} \right| = \left| \mathbb{Z} - \{ 2 \} \right|$. $\blacksquare$
